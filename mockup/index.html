Built around privacy first.

The app is designed so sensitive pension values, OCR results, generated reports, and retirement calculations stay readable only on your device.

  • Appwrite handles identity, signed configuration, device binding, and encrypted backup blobs.
  • Sentry receives scrubbed diagnostics to help fix crashes without collecting financial content.
  • OCR processing is isolated from the marketing website and does not persist source documents.

Clear answers without a sales funnel.
